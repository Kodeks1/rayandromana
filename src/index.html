Location / Travel / Accommodation

The wedding will take place in the south of the Czech Republic in a region called Moravia. It is very close to the Austrian and Slovak borders. Prague is about 2.5 hours away by car and Vienna is about 1.5 hours away.

The reception and accommodation venue is located in Zaječí, while the wedding ceremony will take place in Valtice which is about a 20 minute drive away. We will organise bus transport between the venues.

For guests flying in from overseas, the best bet is to fly into Prague, Brno, or Vienna. EasyJet or Ryan Air can get you there pretty easily from London, just don’t get your hopes up if you’re expecting a spacious seat.

Perhaps the easilest option is to fly to Vienna, and catch a train accross the border to Breclav. We can then organise to have someone pick you by car from there. But let us know your plans, and we’ll figure out the best route for you.
